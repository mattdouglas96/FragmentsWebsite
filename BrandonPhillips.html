Brandon Phillips

Brandon Phillips is a song writer and composer local to Victoria but originally from the city of Burlington. This piano-based performer aspires to pass a message onto the generation that faces the end of the world. After attending school for music production and moving west, inspiration and connection brought a new rise to action. His upcoming album “The Sunshower” will be released in September.

"If you stay in environments where people don't recognize the value of you, you will shrink your gift to the size of what they can stand." — Keion Henderson
