Etianne

Etianne is a Parisian writer, composer and singer. After studying violin, she starts to write poems that later become songs. Inspired by her classical training and her love for electronic music, she develops a sound lulled by melancholia. After a first eclectic album in 2022, she settles down in La Reunion and Canada where she publishes in 2025 an EP called "II".
