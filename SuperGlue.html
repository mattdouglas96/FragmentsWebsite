Super Glue

Super Glue produces his worldly-inspired, multi-genre electronic mixes, remixes, originals and albums. The last 8 years he's been living and djing all over the world: Canada, Czech Republic, New Zealand, Australia, Vietnam, France, USA and Mexico. Throughout his travels & gigs, Super Glue has worked with dancers, live instrumentalists, organized events, busked on the streets, collaborated with multiple styles of creatives and picked up many different music skills along the way. Super Glue is certain to find something for everyone and stick the audience to the dancefloor... And is appropriate for all your venue needs!
